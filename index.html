About

I am a microbiologist with research experience in antimicrobial resistance (AMR), endophytic fungi, and natural product–based drug discovery. I earned my B.Sc. in Microbiology from the University of Ilorin, Nigeria, graduating with a CGPA of 4.68/5.00 and recognized as the Best Graduating Female Student in my department.

My research interests span host–pathogen interactions, immunology, microbial metabolomics, and the application of experimental and computational approaches to address infectious diseases. I am currently seeking MSc and PhD training opportunities in microbiology, infectious diseases, or related biomedical sciences.
